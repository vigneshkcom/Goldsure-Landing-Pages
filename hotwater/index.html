Sizing guide

Which tank is right
for your household?

Getting the sizing right is one of the most important parts of a heat pump install. Here's a simple guide — and if you're unsure, we assess every home before quoting.

290L
Right for you if...
  • 1–4 people in the household
  • Average shower frequency and duration
  • Smaller outdoor installation space
  • Standard hot water usage patterns
330L
Right for you if...
  • 4–6+ people in the household
  • Long showers or frequent baths
  • Solar panels already installed
  • High or unpredictable hot water usage
  • Want maximum capacity and fastest recovery

Yes. From 1 March 2027, Victorian legislation requires that failed gas hot water systems be replaced with electric alternatives. You won't be able to replace a broken gas unit with another gas unit. 2026 is the last year to make this switch on your terms — with full government rebates available and enough time to plan the right system for your home rather than making a rushed emergency decision.
To be eligible for the Solar Victoria hot water rebate you need to be an owner-occupier, have a combined household taxable income under $210,000, and the property must not have previously received a Solar Homes hot water rebate. The VEU discount has no income test and is available to all Victorian households. We confirm your eligibility as part of the quote process.
